Proof of Identity


CASA has recently changed it's ARN policy and now requires all ARN holders to pass a 100 point identification check.

Make sure you have 100 points of identification available to verifty your identity. This may include your passport, drivers licence, Medicare card and bank and credit card statements. See a complete list of what identification is acceptable..

Your identification documents will be verified using a document verification system. If your documents cannot be immediately verified, you will be requested to upload a certified copy of your documents. See our list of who can certify documents.
